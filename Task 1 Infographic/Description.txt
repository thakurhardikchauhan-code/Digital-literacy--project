This poster highlights the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students in today’s technology-driven world. It explains that digital literacy means using the internet, applications, and digital devices safely, responsibly, and effectively. The content emphasizes how these skills support academic success, help maintain online safety, and contribute to building a positive professional image, which is useful for future jobs and internships.
The poster also provides practical guidance on safe internet practices, such as not sharing passwords, avoiding suspicious links, using strong and secure passwords, and ignoring fake messages. In addition, it outlines basic email etiquette, including using clear subject lines, maintaining polite and respectful language, avoiding informal slang, and ending messages professionally. Overall, it encourages students to become smart and responsible digital users.
