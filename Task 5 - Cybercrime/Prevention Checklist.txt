Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone
- Don't approve unknown payment requests
- Always verify before clicking any links
- Use only official & trusted apps
- Enable app lock for payment applications
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Regularly check your bank statements
- Report any suspicious activity immediately

Report at: cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline: 1930
